About the project

Improvement and infrastructure of the complex

The houses are designed in high-tech style with an emphasis on intelligent and efficient use of space.

Reliable house designs are earthquake-resistant and comply with all safety standards and requirements. The facades of the house, modern in shape and color palette, combine the color of natural pearls and gray anthracite.
