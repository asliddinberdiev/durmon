5 Easy Steps to Buying an Apartment

1. Appointment

You can preview the projects on our website, social media pages, or order a call back right away. A personal manager will contact you by phone or Telegram messenger and set a meeting time at the sales office.

2. Selecting an apartment to buy

At the sales office, our specialist will provide you with complete information on all the projects that interest you: he will check the availability of apartments and their prices, select the appropriate layouts and apartment options that are right for you.

3. Collection of documents

As soon as you choose a suitable apartment, you will be provided with a purchase and sale agreement for review, for the preparation of which you only need a passport and TIN.

4. Signing and payment of the contract

After signing the agreement, if necessary, our employee will take you to the bank, where you can make payment under the agreement by depositing funds through the bank’s cash desk or paying through the Internet banking system.

5. Delivery of the house and registration of ownership

After successful registration of the contract, you can receive ownership documents and keys to your new apartment. At this stage, you become the legal owner of your home!
