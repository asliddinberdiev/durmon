About the project

Improvement and infrastructure of the complex

The houses are designed in high-tech style with an emphasis on intelligent and efficient use of space.

Reliable house designs are earthquake-resistant and comply with all safety standards and requirements. The facades of the house, modern in shape and color palette, combine the color of natural pearls and gray anthracite.

Large windows

Large windows are a real decoration of the apartment. Lots of natural light creates a feeling of spaciousness in the apartment and visually increases the interior space.

Architecture

The houses are designed in a high-tech style with an emphasis on the reasonable and rational use of space.

The reliable structures of the houses are earthquake-resistant and comply with all safety standards and requirements.

Thoughtful entrance areas

The reception area is located on the first floor. And for the convenience of families with small children, we have designed a spacious stroller room, where you can leave the stroller until the next walk.

Three silent high-speed elevators in the entrance will allow you to quickly and safely get to your floor.

Underground parking

If you have a car, you no longer need to think about where to park it. The project of the house for residents provides underground parking, equipped with access control and video surveillance.

To get home, you do not have to go outside. You can go up to your floor by elevator directly from the parking lot.
